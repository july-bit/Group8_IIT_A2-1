Software Engineer

Image 1: Rahul Pandey - Staff Software Engineer

What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

According to Webster, the main responsibilities of software engineers involve:

  • System Design: create the outline of the web applications and products and provide data for the software developer to complete the project.
  • Establish New Documentation: create the documentation to help users understand the functionality of the products.
  • Maintain Software Functionality: update the product to meet current industry standards, diagnose issues and come up with new ideas for the future improvement.
  • Troubleshoot and Upgrade Existing Systems: tackle the potential problems and maintain the systems.
  • Comply with Company and Industry Standards: keep up with industry trends and guarantee that the systems meet industry requirements.
    • (Webster 2021)

    Rahul Pandey, a Staff Software Engineer at Facebook, has been working in Silicon Valley for eight years. He's also a professor teaching an Android class at Stanford University, and a Youtuber who posts coding interview videos. Pandey is also a product engineer whose responsibilities include both frontend and backend of the products. As he mentioned, half of his day is to do technical chores such as reading, writing, and modifying codes, and submitting GitHub pull requests. He spends the other half of his time reviewing architecture, assisting people, unblocking them, and providing other ways to tackle the problems. (Mihailescu, C 2021)

    What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

    Rahul Pandey enjoys connecting with others, and his job requires him to work as part of a team and engage with other professionals such as the engineering manager, product manager, and designer to develop a strategy for the entire team. As a professor, he has to interact with his students at Stanford University. He also makes videos on Youtuber to communicate and teach other people about coding. (Mihailescu, C 2021)

    Where do the IT professionals spend most of their time?

    Rahul Pandey did not share where he spent most of his time. However, as a professor, he could spend part of his time at Stanford University and the rest of his time in front of his table doing technical things as a staff software engineer. (Mihailescu, C 2021)

    What aspect of their position is most challenging?

    Rahul Pandey shared about his year-long project that his team had to integrate the architecture made by another team in the company into their product. His duty is to examine the design and try to figure out the potential problems. Besides the engineering challenge, he has to face marketing and communication challenges such as convincing people who work on the new feature on the architecture and helping others understand the value of this architecture. (Mihailescu, C 2021)
